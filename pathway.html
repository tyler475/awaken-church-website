The Awaken Pathway

Four steps. One direction. No matter where you are on your journey, there's a clear next step waiting for you at Awaken.

01
Step One

Start Here

New to Awaken? Start Here is our monthly lunch for first-time guests and anyone who wants to know more about who we are, what we believe, and how to get connected. Meet Pastor Tyler and Kayla, hear the story of Awaken, and find your place in this family.

It's free, casual, and there's food. No pressure, no commitment — just a chance to get to know us.

Register for Start Here
02
Step Two

Saved Here

Whether you gave your life to Christ at Awaken or you're ready to make your faith public through water baptism, this step is for you. We believe baptism is a powerful declaration of what God has done in your life — and we'd love to celebrate that moment with you.

Talk to one of our team members on a Sunday or fill out the form below and we'll reach out to you personally.

I'm Ready for Baptism
03
Step Three

Serve Here

Everyone at Awaken has a role to play. Serve Here is how you find yours. Whether it's on the welcome team, in kids ministry, on the worship team, or behind the scenes — there's a spot for you and it matters more than you know.

We'll help you discover your gifts and connect you with a team where you'll thrive.

Find Your Serve Spot
04
Step Four

Stay Here

Membership at Awaken isn't a box to check — it's a covenant. It's saying "I'm all in for this family and this mission." Members commit to the vision, grow together in community, and take responsibility for each other's spiritual health.

If Awaken is your church home, make it official. We'd love nothing more.

Become a Member
