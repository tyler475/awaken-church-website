Our Story

We're Glad You Found Us

Awaken Church launched in January 2022 in Cartersville, GA with one mission — to help people Know God, Find Freedom, and Live On Purpose.

Who We Are

A Church Family,
Not a Corporation

We're an ARC/CMN-affiliated church plant in Bartow County averaging around 180 people across two Sunday services. We believe church should feel like family — a place where everyone has a name, not a number.

We gather every Sunday at 9:30am and 11:00am at 801 West Ave, Cartersville, GA.

What We Believe

Our Core Beliefs

We're a Spirit-filled church rooted in the authority of Scripture. Here's what we stand on.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our ultimate authority for life and faith.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone — His death, burial, and resurrection.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit and the ongoing work of the Spirit in the life of every believer.

The Church

We believe the local church is God's primary vehicle for advancing His kingdom in the earth.

Baptism

We practice believer's baptism by immersion as a public declaration of faith in Jesus Christ.

The Return of Christ

We believe in the literal, physical return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of the dead.

What to Expect

Your First Sunday

When you arrive, our parking team will help you find a spot — first-time guests get front-row parking. Inside you'll find free coffee, friendly faces, and a welcome team ready to help you get connected. Services run about 75 minutes with energetic worship and real, practical teaching from God's Word.
