Lead Pastors

Tyler & Kayla

Harris

For over twelve years, Tyler and Kayla Harris have walked side by side in ministry, serving in churches across the United States. Each assignment, each challenge, and each opportunity was God’s way of preparing them — shaping their hearts and stirring a vision to plant a life-giving church.

That vision came alive in January of 2022, when they, along with their three children, Weston, Easton, and Blakelyn, stepped out in faith to launch Awaken Church. Today, Tyler leads with a passion for the gospel, bringing Scripture to life through personal experience, heartfelt vulnerability, and contagious humor.
